About the Field Workshop Program

This Field Workshop brings together researchers, designers, artists, and community practitioners to examine drought adaptation across the San Luis Valley and the Taos Plateau — working at the intersections of hydrology, land governance, ecology, and culture.

The three volumes below form a curriculum: a handbook on integrative methods, a field guide to the regional context, and a (forthcoming) workbook of exercises and reflections for field researchers.
