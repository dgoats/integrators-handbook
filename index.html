Designing for
Adaptation in a
Time of Prolonged Drought

Field Workshop · 2026
San Luis Valley, Colorado & Taos Plateau, New Mexico

This Field Workshop brings together researchers, designers, artists, and community practitioners to examine drought adaptation across the San Luis Valley and the Taos Plateau. Working at the intersections of hydrology, land governance, ecology, and culture, participants develop integrative research and design responses grounded in place.

The three volumes below form the complete curriculum: a handbook on integrative methods, a field guide to the two landscapes, and a workbook of exercises and reflections.
